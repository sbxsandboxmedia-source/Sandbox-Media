01

Purpose and Scope

This Refund Policy applies to purchases made directly from Sandbox Media for professional services, custom projects, consultations and digital products. A signed proposal, invoice or product-specific licence may contain additional terms and will apply together with this policy.

02

Custom Services and Projects

Advance payments reserve project time, research, planning and production capacity. Once discovery, planning, design, development, writing, marketing setup or other work has begun, payments for completed work and reserved capacity are normally non-refundable.

If a project is cancelled before work begins, Sandbox Media may review the request after deducting payment-gateway charges, taxes, consultation fees or other non-recoverable costs.

03

Digital Products and Software

Digital products, templates, source files, dashboards, themes, software access and downloadable resources are generally non-refundable after delivery, download, licence activation or access is provided.

A verified technical defect may be resolved through repair, replacement, setup support or another reasonable solution before a refund is considered.

04

Eligible Refund Situations

  • A confirmed duplicate payment.
  • A service that Sandbox Media accepts but is unable to begin or deliver.
  • A material billing error verified by our team.
  • Another situation expressly stated in the applicable proposal or product terms.
05

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after work, delivery or access has started.
  • Delays caused by missing content, credentials, approvals or client feedback.
  • Dissatisfaction caused by preferences outside the agreed scope.
  • Third-party fees, domains, hosting, plugins, advertising spend, payment charges or licences.
  • Results affected by search engines, advertising platforms, marketplaces or other third parties.
06

Cancellations and Paused Projects

Cancellation requests must be sent in writing. Work completed up to the cancellation date remains payable. Projects paused because of missing feedback or materials may be rescheduled and may require a restart or reactivation fee.

07

Revisions and Scope Changes

Reasonable revisions included in the agreed package are not grounds for a refund. Additional concepts, modules, pages, revisions or changes outside the approved scope may be quoted separately.

08

Refund Request Process

Email hello@sandboxmedia.in with the invoice number, payment date, service or product name, reason for the request and supporting details. We may request further information before making a decision.

09

Processing Time

Approved refunds are generally initiated within 7–10 business days. Banks, payment gateways and card networks may require additional time to complete the credit. Processing charges and taxes may be non-refundable where they cannot be recovered.

10

Contact

Questions about cancellation, eligibility or billing can be sent through our contact page or by email to hello@sandboxmedia.in.

Important notice

This website policy is a general business template and is not legal advice. Final terms should be reviewed for your actual services, payment process and applicable laws.

Need Clarification?

Questions about a payment or cancellation?

Contact Sandbox Media before purchasing or cancelling so we can explain the applicable terms.
