01

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files or similar technologies stored on a device when a website is visited. They can remember preferences, support security, measure performance and help embedded services function.

02

How We Use Cookies

Sandbox Media may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, maintain security, remember limited preferences, understand website performance and improve content and services.

03

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies support core functions such as page navigation, fraud prevention, security and form operation. Blocking them may cause parts of the website to stop working correctly.

04

Analytics and Performance

Analytics tools may collect information such as pages visited, approximate location, device type, browser, referral source and interaction patterns. This information is generally used in aggregated form to improve the website.

05

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies may remember choices such as language, form preferences or interface settings. These cookies are used only if the relevant feature is enabled.

06

Marketing Technologies

If advertising or remarketing tools are enabled in the future, they may use cookies or identifiers to measure campaigns and show more relevant advertising. Such tools should be activated only with appropriate notice or consent where required.

07

Third-Party Services

Google Maps, video platforms, analytics providers, WhatsApp, social media, payment gateways and other embedded services may set or read their own cookies. Their processing is governed by their respective policies.

08

Managing Cookies

You can block, delete or limit cookies through your browser. Common browsers provide controls under privacy, security or site settings. Disabling cookies may affect maps, forms, saved preferences and other features.

09

Cookie Retention

Some cookies are deleted when the browser closes, while others remain for a defined period. Retention depends on the purpose and the provider that sets the cookie.

10

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated when technologies, services or legal requirements change. The latest version and update date will appear on this page.

11
